Thanks for stopping by!

Always an intuitive individual, I see through others in a way that no one else does. Through this ability, I can connect to people immediately and experience their emotions, struggles, and more importantly their needs. It is only logical that I found myself majoring in human-computer interaction, what I believe to be the perfect place for an empath, like myself.

I strongly believe in working in a positive team environment, which I have helped build at the research lab where I currently work. I take on many roles there such as lab manager, user experience designer, user researcher and research participant recruiter.

I am currently doing research on social network analysis and am writing my undergraduate thesis on the visualization of student's social networks and how they change over time.
