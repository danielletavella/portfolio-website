This presentation embodies the work I did for a Statistical Methods for the Cognitive and Behavioral Sciences course I am required to take. I chose the research topic shown in the presentation based on my own experiences with romantic toxic relationships. I was in an abusive relationship with a narcissist for seven years and I have made it my mission to educate young women on warning signs and how to avoid such people. Due to the short time frame of the class, there are many limitations to the research but I would like to continue this research in the hopes of designing a software tool for young women in recovery.



This work was done for my Human Factors and Ergonomics class. My final project was to research a system that needed improvement. I chose to focus on technology that could reduce drunk driving accidents. I concentrated on wearable technology and how it could help to monitor alcohol intake.



This presentation was used for my Social Psychology class where I reviewed a scientific journal pertaining to the relationship among Black CEO's and their perceived babyfaceness.
